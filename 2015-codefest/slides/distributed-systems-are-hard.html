Distributed Systems are HARD

  • Node failures are *inevitable*. Not if it fails, but what to do when it does so (Fault Tolerance).
  • More expensive: to write software and to maintain infrastructure
  • Node coordiantion is not trivial (it is HARD)
  • To build HA system, you have to design your apps appropriately
